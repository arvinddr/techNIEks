Spectrum of Life

Life comes in many spectrums, showing us different perspectives of the world. Colors are very important in one's life as they directly influence one's well-being and mood. Colors have the power to transform a dull ball of rock into a vivid jewel of the colors. Each color has a fascinating story to tell. Different colors can hold various connotations in culture,language and society. Thus spectrum of life aims at portraying the influence of color on the aspects on life.
